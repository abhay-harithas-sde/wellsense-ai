# WellSense AI Platform - Slide Design Guide
## Visual Design Specifications for Task 5.3 Slides

This guide provides specific design instructions for creating the slides in PowerPoint or Google Slides.

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors:
- **Health Blue**: `#4A90E2` (RGB: 74, 144, 226)
- **Wellness Green**: `#7ED321` (RGB: 126, 211, 33)
- **Trust Navy**: `#2C3E50` (RGB: 44, 62, 80)

### Accent Colors:
- **Energy Orange**: `#F5A623` (RGB: 245, 166, 35)
- **Calm Teal**: `#50E3C2` (RGB: 80, 227, 194)
- **Alert Red**: `#E74C3C` (RGB: 231, 76, 60)

### Neutral Colors:
- **Background White**: `#FFFFFF` (RGB: 255, 255, 255)
- **Light Gray**: `#F8F9FA` (RGB: 248, 249, 250)
- **Text Dark**: `#2C3E50` (RGB: 44, 62, 80)
- **Text Medium**: `#7F8C8D` (RGB: 127, 140, 141)

---

## Typography

### Headings:
- **Font**: Montserrat Bold or Poppins Bold
- **Size**: 44pt for slide titles, 36pt for section headers
- **Color**: Trust Navy (#2C3E50)
- **Style**: Bold, sentence case

### Body Text:
- **Font**: Open Sans Regular or Roboto Regular
- **Size**: 24pt for main content, 20pt for details
- **Color**: Text Dark (#2C3E50)
- **Line Height**: 1.5x

### Emphasis:
- **Font**: Open Sans Semibold or Roboto Medium
- **Color**: Health Blue (#4A90E2) or Wellness Green (#7ED321)
- **Style**: Semibold

### Design Elements:

**Flowchart Boxes:**
- Size: 200px wide √ó 80px tall
- Border: 3px solid Health Blue (#4A90E2)
- Background: White with subtle gradient
- Border Radius: 10px
- Shadow: Subtle drop shadow (2px, 2px, 5px, rgba(0,0,0,0.1))

**Arrows:**
- Color: Health Blue (#4A90E2)
- Width: 4px
- Style: Solid with arrow head
- Length: 30px between boxes

**Icons:**
- Size: 32px √ó 32px
- Style: Flat, modern, consistent set
- Color: Health Blue or Wellness Green
- Position: Left side of text in each box

**Right Panel Content:**
- Background: Light Gray (#F8F9FA)
- Padding: 30px
- Border Radius: 15px
- Contains: "What You'll Experience" bullet points

### Animation Sequence:
1. Title fades in (0.5s)
2. Each step appears sequentially from top to bottom (0.3s each)
3. Right panel fades in after all steps (0.5s)
4. Total animation time: ~3 seconds

## Animation Guidelines

### Entrance Animations:
- **Type**: Fade in, slide in from left/right
- **Duration**: 0.3-0.5 seconds
- **Timing**: Sequential for lists, simultaneous for groups
- **Easing**: Ease-out for natural feel

### Emphasis Animations:
- **Type**: Subtle pulse, grow slightly
- **Duration**: 0.2 seconds
- **Use**: Sparingly, only for key points

### Transition Animations:
- **Type**: Fade, push, or none
- **Duration**: 0.5 seconds
- **Consistency**: Same transition throughout

### Avoid:
- ‚ùå Spinning, bouncing, or distracting animations
- ‚ùå Sound effects
- ‚ùå Overly long animations (>1 second)
- ‚ùå Different animations for similar elements

## Accessibility Considerations

### Color Contrast:
- **Text on Background**: Minimum 4.5:1 ratio
- **Large Text**: Minimum 3:1 ratio
- **Test**: Use WebAIM Contrast Checker

### Font Sizes:
- **Minimum**: 18pt for body text
- **Recommended**: 20-24pt for readability
- **Titles**: 36-48pt

### Alternative Text:
- Add alt text to all images and icons
- Describe charts and diagrams verbally

### Readability:
- Avoid all-caps for long text
- Use sentence case for better readability
- Ensure sufficient line spacing (1.5x)

- **Format**: .pptx (editable) and .pdf (backup)
