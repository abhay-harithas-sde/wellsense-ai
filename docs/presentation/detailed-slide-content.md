## Presentation Delivery Tips

### For All Speakers:

1. **Energy & Enthusiasm**: Show passion for the project
2. **Eye Contact**: Look at audience, not just slides
3. **Pace**: Speak clearly, not too fast
4. **Transitions**: Smooth handoffs between speakers
5. **Body Language**: Stand confidently, use gestures naturally

### For Demo Operator (Team Member 4):

1. **Practice**: Rehearse the exact clicks and navigation
2. **Narrate**: Explain what you're doing as you do it
3. **Backup**: Know how to switch to video instantly
4. **Timing**: Keep strict 5-minute limit
5. **Confidence**: If something glitches, stay calm and use backup

### For Q&A:

1. **Listen**: Fully understand the question before answering
2. **Clarify**: Repeat or rephrase if needed
3. **Concise**: Keep answers brief and focused
4. **Honest**: If you don't know, say so and offer to follow up
5. **Team**: Different members can answer different questions
